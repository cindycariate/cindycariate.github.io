Title: A Database for Items Sold in the Carabao Center

The Carabao Center at Caraga State University is dedicated to researching, developing, and promoting technologies related to carabao. This project aims to establish a centralized database for the Carabao Center. This database will manage information regarding items sold and the transaction process.
